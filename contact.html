Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Smart Hire

How do I get started?

Simply click "Get Started" and create your account. You can start posting jobs immediately after registration. No credit card required for the free trial.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. Experience the complete platform before making a decision.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@smarthire.com or through the contact form above. We respond within 24 hours.
